Server Rules

Please follow these rules to keep our community safe and fun!

🚫

No Griefing

Griefing is strictly prohibited. This includes destroying or stealing from other players' builds, stealing items, or causing intentional damage to claimed land. Use /report to notify staff of griefers.

⚠️

No Hacking

The use of hacks, cheats, x-ray, fly hacks, speed hacks, or any form of unauthorized modification is strictly forbidden. This includes using bugs or glitches to gain unfair advantages.

💬

Respect Players

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, bullying, discrimination, or toxic behavior. Be kind and welcoming to new players. Use /ignore to block problematic players.

🔇

No Spam

Do not spam chat, repeat messages, use excessive caps, or advertise other servers. Keep chat clean and readable. Use the appropriate chat channels for your messages.

🏗️

Respect Claimed Land

Only build on unclaimed land or within your own claims. Always get permission before building on someone else's claim. Use /trust to give others access to your claim.

💰

No Scamming

Scamming other players out of items, money, or ranks is prohibited. All trades should be conducted fairly. Staff will not reimburse scammed items.

🎃

No AFK Machines

Creating AFK machines or using AFK farms to gain unfair advantages is not allowed. Players may go AFK but must not exploit game mechanics.

📢

Follow Staff Instructions

Always follow the instructions given by server staff. Their decisions are final. If you have issues, use /report or contact staff through Discord.

⚠️

Violation Consequences

Breaking any of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the offense. Severe or repeated violations will lead to permanent bans without refund of purchased items.
