Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common store questions

How do I purchase items from the store?

To purchase items:

  1. Click the "Buy Now" button on the item you want
  2. You'll be redirected to our secure payment portal
  3. Choose your payment method (UPI, Credit Card, etc.)
  4. Complete the payment process
  5. Your purchase will be delivered within 24 hours
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm)
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • PayPal (for international payments)
How long does it take to receive my purchase?

Most purchases are delivered within 24 hours. Here's what to expect:

  • Ranks and cosmetics: Delivered within 1-12 hours
  • Discord perks: Delivered within 1-6 hours
  • Bundles: Delivered within 6-24 hours

If you haven't received your purchase after 24 hours, please contact our support team.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Full refunds are available within 48 hours of purchase if the item hasn't been delivered
  • Partial refunds may be available in special circumstances
  • No refunds for delivered digital items unless there's a technical issue we can't resolve

To request a refund, please contact our support team with your purchase details.

Do purchases expire or need renewal?

Most of our items are permanent purchases:

  • Ranks: Permanent (one-time payment)
  • Cosmetics: Permanent (one-time payment)
  • Discord Booster: Monthly subscription
  • In-game coins: Permanent until spent

Any items with expiration dates will be clearly marked before purchase.
