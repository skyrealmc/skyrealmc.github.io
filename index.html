Help Center

Find answers to common questions

How do I join the server?

To join our server, follow these steps:

  1. Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition (version 1.21 or newer)
  2. Go to the "Servers" tab
  3. Click "Add Server"
  4. Enter the server address: skyrealm.run.place
  5. Enter the port: 4173
  6. Click "Save" and then "Join Server"
I'm having connection issues. What should I do?

If you're having trouble connecting:

  • Make sure you're using the correct IP and port
  • Check that your Minecraft version is up to date
  • Restart your game and device
  • Try connecting at a different time (server may be restarting)
  • Join our Discord for real-time support
How does the economy system work?

Our server features a player-driven economy:

  • Earn coins by selling items at spawn or completing quests
  • Use coins to buy items from other players or the server shop
  • Create your own shop with chests using our shop plugin
  • Participate in events for bonus coins and rewards
How do I claim land to protect my builds?

To claim land:

  1. use !land claim command
  2. Select two opposite corners of the area you want to claim
  3. stand the first corner with the land claim command
  4. after go to the opposite corner to claim the land
  5. Your land is now protected from griefing(land claim cost in-game currency)

Note: You can expand claims as you rich

How do I report a player or issue?

To report issues:

  • In-game: Use !report [player] [reason] or use compass menu
  • Discord: Open a ticket in our #support channel
  • Provide evidence (screenshots/videos) when possible
  • Our staff team will investigate promptly
